How to play Tix-Tac-Toe

  • Each player has multiple attempts to strike until the boxes are filled
  • Each attempt count will swap between you and the opponent
  • By default, the game is played on only 1 round but can add up to 3 or 5 rounds
    • 1 Round (default) only consists of one match
    • 3 Rounds goes up to 3 matches
    • 5 Rounds goes up to 5 matches
  • Wait until your opponent makes a move
  • Any chosen symbols should align in a single straight line with 3 of that symboles
